The Problem We're Solving

Open any popular SIP calculator in India. Enter ₹10,000/month, 12% return, 20 years. It tells you: ₹99.9 Lakh corpus. Sounds amazing, right?

Here's what it doesn't tell you:

  • 💸 LTCG Tax: You'll owe 12.5% tax on gains above ₹1.25 lakh per year — reducing your take-home significantly
  • 📉 Inflation: At 6% annual inflation, that ₹99.9 lakh in 2044 is worth only about ₹31 lakh in today's money
  • 🏦 Expense Ratio: A 1% expense ratio silently reduces your 12% return to 11% — costing you lakhs over 20 years

Financial influencers on YouTube and Instagram routinely show the big nominal number. We think you deserve the real one.

📊

Example: A ₹10,000/month SIP at 12% for 20 years shows ₹99.9L nominal. After LTCG tax and 6% inflation, the real value in today's purchasing power is closer to ₹28–32 lakh. That's still great — but it's the truth you need to plan with.
